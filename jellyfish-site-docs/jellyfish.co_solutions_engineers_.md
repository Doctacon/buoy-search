### Improve 1:1s and Growth

Prepare for coaching conversations with clear context, actionable insights, and historical trends on your work and impact.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-share-feedback-devex.webp)

### Share Feedback to Drive Change

Tell leadership where work is blocked and what tools or support you need so your team can work smarter and faster.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-lifecycle-explorer-update.webp)

### Improve Team Collaboration

Use workflow, capacity, and feedback trends to identify what works best and help your team collaborate more effectively.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-work-in-flight.webp)

### Avoid Burnout

Know your team’s capacity and priorities so you can focus on meaningful work without overloading yourself.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-jf-assistant.webp)

### Get Instant Answers

Use Jellyfish Assistant to quickly understand progress, priorities, and blockers without waiting for meetings.
