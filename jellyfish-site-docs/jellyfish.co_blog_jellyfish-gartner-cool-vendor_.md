May 11, 2021

We are excited and proud to share that Gartner has named Jellyfish a [2021 Cool Vendor for Software Engineering Technologies](https://www.gartner.com/en/documents/4001232).

Gartner’s definition of a Cool Vendor is a small company offering a technology or service that is:

- **Innovative** — Enables users to do things they couldn’t do before.
- **Impactful** — Has or will have a business impact, not just technology for its own sake.
- **Intriguing** — Has caught Gartner’s interest during the past six months.

For the Software Engineering Technologies category, Gartner selects companies that enable engineering teams to move faster and provide increased visibility into business value—the very mission of Jellyfish. We’ve set out to help engineering teams maximize the business impact of engineering, all while optimizing operations to deliver faster. This recognition validates what many of our existing customers like SessionM, GoodRx, and Toast have already realized.

We are honored to be recognized as a Gartner Cool Vendor. We will continue to be a technology provider that drives innovation for engineering teams and a pioneer in elevating engineering leadership.
