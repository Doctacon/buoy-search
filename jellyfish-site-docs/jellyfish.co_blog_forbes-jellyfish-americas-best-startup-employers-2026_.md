We’re excited to announce that Jellyfish is named among Forbes and Statista’s ranking of [America’s Best Startup Employers 2026](https://www.forbes.com/lists/americas-best-startup-employers/).

The list recognizes the top-performing startups in the United States for employer reputation, employee satisfaction, and company growth. The organizers gathered more than 7 million data points from over 20,000 eligible companies, then conducted in-depth analysis on 2,700 employers. Jellyfish is one of just 500 employers included in the final ranking from Forbes.

![Jellyfish team](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Jellyfish_Forbes-Americas-Best-Startup-Employers-2026_Inlien-Image.webp)

“We’re thrilled to be recognized as one of America’s best startup employers,” said Alexandra LaMaster, Chief People Officer at Jellyfish. “We’d like to thank our employees, as well as the engineers and engineering leaders around the world who use our platform, for making this award possible. A commitment to innovation, openness to new ideas, and collaborative spirit run through the Jellyfish community, and those qualities add up to an organization in which people appreciate both the work they do and the team they work with.”

This recognition is a result of staying true to our values: swimming against the current, glowing brightly, and blooming together. We’re dedicated to building a company and a culture where our employees and their families can thrive. Our team is fueled by innovation and helping AI-integrated engineering teams drive measurable impact and better business outcomes.

![Jellyfish Values](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Jellyfish_Forbes-Americas-Best-Startup-Employers-2026_Inlien-Company-Values.webp)

To that end, we recently launched the [Jellyfish AI Engineering Trends](https://jellyfish.co/ai-engineering-trends/) benchmark site, the largest collection of quantitative analyses of its kind, designed to share with the community what we’re learning about AI transformation in the real world. We also recently announced our [partnership with Augment Code](https://jellyfish.co/blog/ai-impact-augment-code/) to help organizations prove AI’s strategic value to the business.
