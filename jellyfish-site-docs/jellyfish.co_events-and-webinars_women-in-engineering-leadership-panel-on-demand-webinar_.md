# Women in Engineering Leadership Panel

Companies all over the world have made strides toward creating a more diverse and inclusive working environment, yet there’s still progress to be made. At Jellyfish we believe in celebrating diversity of all forms. In honor of International Women’s Day, we hosted a live Women in Engineering Leadership Panel on Tuesday, March 8th at 1pm EST.

We heard from distinguished female engineering leaders about how they got started in their field, lessons learned, and their perspective on paving the way for the future of women in engineering.

###### Joining us on the panel was:

- Debra Brucato, Senior Director of Engineering Operations, SessionM
- Sarojini Tati, CTO and VP of Engineering Operations, DemandTec by Acoustic
- Indra Klavins, Engineering Operations Director, Automox
