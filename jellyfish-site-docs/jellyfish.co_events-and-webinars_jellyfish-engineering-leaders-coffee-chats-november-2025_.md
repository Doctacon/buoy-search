**The AI Mirror: Reflections on DORA 2025**

[The 2025 DORA State of AI-Assisted Software Development report](https://jellyfish.co/blog/dora-2025-infographic/) marks a turning point in the evolution of engineering leadership in the age of AI. After years of experimentation, hype, and growing pains, we’re beginning to see the first signs of maturity in how teams are adopting, adapting to, and benefiting from AI across the software development lifecycle.

But what’s most striking about this year’s findings isn’t just that AI is everywhere – it’s that AI acts like a mirror. It reflects and amplifies your organization’s true engineering strengths and weaknesses.

Join us on November 14 at 12 PM ET to discuss and unpack what the AI mirror means for engineering managers, leadership and their teams as AI continues to reshape productivity and team dynamics.
