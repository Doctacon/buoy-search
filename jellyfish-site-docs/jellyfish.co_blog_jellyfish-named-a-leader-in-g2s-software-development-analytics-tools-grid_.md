In this article

We’re excited to announce that for the 9th consecutive quarter, Jellyfish has been named a Leader in [G2’s Software Development Analytics Tools Grid](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools#grid). We are thrilled to maintain both our overall leadership position in the grid as well as the top spot in user Satisfaction Ratings. These rankings attest to Jellyfish’s constant innovation and commitment to customer health and happiness as well as overall leadership in the [software engineering intelligence](https://www.jellyfish.co/) space.

Jellyfish has also maintained its top position in the Mid-Market Relationship Index for Software Development Analytics Tools, achieving a score of 9.42/10 – the highest in the category. The Relationship Index reflects:

- **Ease of Doing Business:** How easy it is to work with Jellyfish, based on reviews by G2 users
- **Quality of Support:** Satisfaction with the quality of our customer support
- **Likelihood to Recommend:** Customers’ likelihood to recommend Jellyfish
- **Number of Reviews:** More reviews mean greater trust and indicate a more representative and accurate reflection of the customer experience

This recognition underscores the trust our customers place in Jellyfish and our continuous commitment to delivering a platform that drives real business impact.

You can download the [_Fall 2024 Grid Report here_](https://www.jellyfish.co/resources/g2-grid-report-fall-2024/) for more details.

Leading the Way in User Satisfaction

## Leading the Way in User Satisfaction

We’re thrilled to be the overall leader in Software Development Analytics Tools, but what matters most to us is the opinions of our customers. Which is why our position as leader in _Satisfaction_ among products in the Software Development Analytics Tools category is especially exciting.

Here’s what our users are saying:

- 98% of users rated us 4 or 5 stars
- 94% believe Jellyfish is headed in the right direction
- 90% are likely to recommend Jellyfish to others

These stats are a reflection of our dedication to providing a platform that ensures alignment throughout the entire engineering organization. With Jellyfish, thousands of users around the world are better able to anticipate the future, build with efficiency, and deliver business impact.
