## The Jellyfish Values

Everyone at Jellyfish contributes to our culture, and works to create an awesome place where we can all thrive.

![JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Swim Against The Current](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Swim-Against-The-Current.svg)

Swim against the current

Be courageous, innovative and differentiate.

We go for bold solutions with big impact on business outcomes. We speak up, value diverse perspectives, and do the right thing even when it’s hard.

![JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Swim Against The Current](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Swim-Against-The-Current.svg)

Swim against the current

Jet fast, but don’t wreck

Activate quickly but with intent and forethought.

We have the grit to produce and make timely decisions – even without perfect data. We raise and resolve issues together and keep moving forward.

![JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric Seaweed-F_Value_Jet Fast But Dont Wreck Things](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Jet-Fast-But-Dont-Wreck-Things.svg)

Glow brightly

Learn, teach, and share openly.

We help each other get better at what we do through asking thoughtful questions and sharing wisdom. We learn together and believe that good ideas can come from anyone anywhere.

![JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric Seaweed-F_Value_Glow Brightly](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Glow-Brightly.svg)

Surf to morph

See ahead, adapt, and act in the right moment.

We notice patterns, stay self-aware, and shift when needed. We embrace ambiguity, experimentation, and lean into ongoing evolution.

![JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric Seaweed-F_Value_Surf To Morph](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Outline-Electric-Seaweed-F_Value_Surf-To-Morph.svg)

Build culture, team cohesion, and human connection.

We cultivate trust, inclusion, and effective interactions. We see each other as humans first, assume good intent, and create a welcoming experience for all.

## We care for our people

We’re dedicated to building a company and a culture where our employees and their families can thrive. This means creating a remarkable employee experience and offering great benefits and perks.


![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Identity-_-Access-Managment.svg)

##### Work Environment

Jellies live all over the US and our HQ is in Boston, MA. We’re committed to being a flexible workplace. We care more about getting the work done and learning from each other than the location you’re in to do it!

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Flexible-Time-Off.svg)

##### Time Off

Time away from work is essential for everyone, which is why we offer Flexible Time Off so you can take the time when you need it. Plus we have 13 company holidays.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Equity.svg)

##### Equity

On top of a competitive salary, we offer stock options so you can be a part of Jellyfish’s future success. We want you to have a stake in what we’re creating!

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Parental-Leave.svg)

##### Paid Parental Leave

Bringing a new family member home is an exciting time, and we want you to be fully present for it. We offer paid parental leave to help you care for your loved ones during this special time. Parents also support one another in our dedicated #parents Slack channel.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Pet-Insurance.svg)

##### Pet Insurance

Take care of your furry family members through our two different pet insurance plans. We are pawsitive that these plans are the purr-fect safety net for your pets!

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Outings-Events.svg)

##### Outings and Events

We’re here to both work and play! Team dinners, Red Sox games, company outings, and game nights are some of the great ways we bond with our colleagues.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Retirement-Savings-Plan.svg)

##### 401(k) and Financial Wellbeing

Our 401K plan has pre-tax and Roth contribution options to encourage saving for retirement. We have access to financial wellness resources through Fidelity and offer free access to financial advisors – including 1 on 1 counseling to help you reach your financial goals.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/JLYFSH-Iconography-Large_Outline-Jelly-Sting-F_Benefit_Health-Benefits.svg)

##### Health Insurance & Ancillary Benefits

Your health is incredibly important (to you and to us!) and Jellyfish is proud to offer top-tier medical and dental plans to keep you and your family in good health.
