# The Jellyfish Guide to Engineering Metrics

CEOs, CFOs and board members are increasingly frustrated by their inability to measure and understand the organization’s engineering work. Other areas of the business are expected to answer for where their money is going. It’s no longer acceptable for a CTO or an engineering leader to throw up their hands and say that engineering is too nuanced to measure.

In this eBook, we’ll look at how the engineering metrics problem evolved, and how you can address it by carefully defining productivity and performance for three specific audiences:

- Your boards of directors
- Your executive peers
- Your engineering teams themselves
