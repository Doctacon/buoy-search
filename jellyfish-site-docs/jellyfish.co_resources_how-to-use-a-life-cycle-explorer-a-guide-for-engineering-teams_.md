# How to use a Life Cycle Explorer: A Guide for Engineering Teams

Engineering teams need an easy way to easily visualize, break down, and understand their development life cycles. Life Cycle Explorers provide teams the ability to investigate the root cause of slowdowns, bottlenecks, and process breakdowns.

In this guide, we break down how to use a Life Cycle Explorer at every stage of your software development life cycle, including:

- Refinement
- Work
- Review & more
