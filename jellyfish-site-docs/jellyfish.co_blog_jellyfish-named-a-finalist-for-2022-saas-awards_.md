August 25, 2022

Jellyfish is honored to be named a finalist in the 2022 [SaaS Awards](https://www.cloud-awards.com/software-as-a-service-awards/) in the “Engineering Management” category.

Administered by the Cloud Awards, the SaaS Awards celebrate innovation and excellence in the software industry. Jellyfish was named a finalist for our pioneering Engineering Management Platform, which provides complete visibility into engineering organizations and how they operate. The data-driven insights we provide help companies understand both where their engineering resources are being spent and how those resources are providing value to the organization.

“As every company becomes a software-centric company, the world will need to turn to engineering leaders for product and business strategy,” said Jellyfish CEO Andrew Lau. “The most effective engineering and product leaders will use data-driven insights to make decisions for their organizations and for their businesses. Being a finalist for the SaaS Awards is a special honor which demonstrates the innovation of our platform and the value we provide for our customers.”

Now in its seventh year, the Software-as-a-Service Awards received entries from hundreds of organizations from across the globe. The full shortlist and finalists in each category can be viewed [here](https://www.cloud-awards.com/2022-saas-awards-finalists/).

The final SaaS Awards winners will be announced September 13, 2022.
