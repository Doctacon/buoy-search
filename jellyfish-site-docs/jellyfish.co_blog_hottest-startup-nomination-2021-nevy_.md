Jellyfish

October 18, 2021

Jellyfish is excited to announce that we have been nominated for the Hottest Startup award at the 2021 NEVY Awards, hosted by the [New England Venture Capital Association (NEVCA)](http://www.newenglandvc.org/) and presented by Dell Technologies. The NEVYs, now in their 9th year, celebrate New England’s top innovators, investors, and companies across the region’s health care, life science, tech, medtech, and cleantech communities. Final winners will be unmasked via a dynamic live broadcast and live event at the House of Blues Boston on December 2, 2021 at 6:30 PM.

Jellyfish is honored to have been named to the shortlist of nominees amongst the innovative companies and organizations in the New England startup ecosystem. We will continue to be a technology provider that drives innovation for engineering teams as well as a supportive member of the growing Boston startup ecosystem.

This news comes after a tremendous year of growth for Jellyfish. In January of this year, Jellyfish [raised $31.5 million in a Series B round](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-gartner-cool-vendor/) of funding led by Insight Partners and joined by previous investors Accel and Wing Venture Capital. Jellyfish was named a [2021 Cool Vendor](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-gartner-cool-vendor/) for Software Engineering Technologies by Gartner in May, and nominated by Hackernoon as one of the [best startups in Boston](https://startups.hackernoon.com/us/boston) in September.

To learn more about the NEVYs, or to join the fun and purchase tickets to the event, visit [thenevys.com](https://thenevys.com/).
