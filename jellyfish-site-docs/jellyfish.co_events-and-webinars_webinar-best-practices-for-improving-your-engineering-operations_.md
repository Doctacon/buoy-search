# Jira Best Practices for Improving Your Engineering Operations

As a leader of a software development organization, you need a deep understanding for the operations of the teams in order to improve efficiencies, and measure the impact of structural, process, and tooling changes that you’ve implemented. For many engineering organizations, the primary tool for tracking the progress of ongoing projects and moving them through the workflow is Jira. And while Jira can be a powerful system, it can be complex and confusing for managing and improving engineering operations. It can be hard to know where to start in organizing Jira to work well for your organization.

Listen to Matt Raimundo, Senior Program Manager, Cloud Operations at Genesys, and Glenn Barnett, Product Leader at Jellyfish and Jira Implementation Expert as they discuss the best practices you can guide your teams to follow in order to improve the development process, streamlining reporting, and ultimately optimizing your organization’s engineering operations.
