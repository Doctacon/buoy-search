# The Essential Guide to Software Delivery Management

#### Understanding the Fundamentals, Methodologies, Tools and Roles to Delivering Software Successfully

Effective software delivery management – overseeing the planning, execution, and delivery of software projects or initiatives – is critical in organizations of all sizes.

In _The Essential Guide to Software Delivery Management_, you’ll learn:

**– Delivery Management Basics:** Understand deliverables, key roles, and common challenges in software delivery.

**– Essential Tools and Metrics:** Discover the best tools and metrics for tracking progress and aligning engineering efforts with business goals.

**– How Jellyfish Helps:** Learn how Jellyfish’s platform enhances visibility, identifies blockers, and enables data-driven decision-making for reliable, efficient delivery.
