We’re thrilled to share that Jellyfish has once again been named a leader by G2 in the Software Development Analytics Tools grid. This positioning marks more than three consecutive years at the top, highlighting our proven track record of consistent innovation, delivery, and strong customer satisfaction.

G2 ranks products using a combination of user review data and public online signals. Scores are calculated based on:

- G2’s Satisfaction algorithms
- User satisfaction
- Review quality
- Product growth (employee, web, and social)
- Year-over-year trends.

Products are categorized through G2’s standardized methodology. Ratings are unbiased, real-time and verified for authenticity.

- 94% believe we’re moving in the right direction
