You’re sharing your latest engineering strategy, but instead of excitement, you’re met with disengagement or confusion from non-engineering colleagues. Sound familiar?

This GLOW 2024 session aims to demystify engineering decisions and strategies, making them not only understandable but also engaging, to significantly enhance project outcomes and strengthen partnerships. We discuss presenting metrics and strategies in a way that clarifies the impact of engineering decisions, inviting feedback and fostering collaboration.
