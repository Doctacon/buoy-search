We’ve got exciting news brewing. Our next Engineering Leader Coffee Chat is happening on **Friday, March 21st** from 12-1 PM EST. Coffee Chats are casual discussions with fellow software engineering leaders serving up experiences, challenges, and best practices – no Jellyfish talk allowed!

This month, we’re stirring up a hot topic: **Driving Innovation During Difficult Economic Times**. We’ll be joined by Gelu Ticala, CTO at Kinaxis, to share how his teams have been able to drive innovation and do more with less in a post-COVID world. Specifically, we’ll dive into the concept of “innovation metrics” and how those can help (or hurt?) engineering teams.

Bring your steaming cup of joe and your hottest takes for a great discussion with fellow engineering leaders.
