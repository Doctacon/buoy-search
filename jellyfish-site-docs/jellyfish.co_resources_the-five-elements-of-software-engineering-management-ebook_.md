# The 5 Elements of Software Engineering Management

Engineering leadership doesn’t need another metric framework; it requires a new perspective. CEOs, senior engineering leaders, managers, and software engineers must shift how they relate their work to business impact.

The 5 Elements of Software Engineering Management is a guide that seeks to provide you with an alternate perspective so you can build engineering organizations that anticipate business needs and deliver innovation that delights customers.

This ebook presents a definitive methodology you can use for:

- Aligning the engineering organization within
- Aligning engineering with the broader business
- How you can shape the new engineering leadership paradigm in order to build trust and enable everyone to do their best work.

This work is a culmination of our collaboration with numerous bright minds in technical leadership to understand what common principles break down organizational barriers and help create elite engineering organizations.
