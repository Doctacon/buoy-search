# Innovation on the Rocks: R&D Efficiency in the Age of AI

October 22nd, 6:45pm – 8:30pm

Mezze on the River, 375 S End Ave, New York, NY 10280

_If you’re attending PEI, Mezze is only a 6-8 minute walk away!_

GenAI is changing the game. Join fellow industry leaders and tech executives to ‘innovate on the rocks’ as you exchange insights on how your teams are tackling the challenges and opportunities of R&D efficiency in the age of AI.

On Tuesday, October 22nd, Jellyfish, [West Monroe](https://www.westmonroe.com/), and [ProsperOps](https://www.prosperops.com/) are partnering for an exclusive Executive Networking Reception hosted at the [Mezze on the River](https://mezzeontheriver.com/).

The evening will feature networking, drinks, and dynamic conversations focused on leveraging AI to optimize R&D processes. You’ll hear firsthand insights from R&D leaders about their experiences navigating the evolving landscape of AI and efficiency, as well as the challenges they’ve faced and the innovative solutions they’ve implemented.

We encourage you to extend the invitation to a local executive peer who appreciates great food, drinks, and meaningful networking.

Form will no longer be available once capacity is reached, please be sure to secure your spot.

Reach out to [events@jellyfish.co](mailto:events@jellyfish.co) for any additional inquiries.
