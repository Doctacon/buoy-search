# Zendesk Integration

Sync Zendesk tickets and service requests to track support workload and performance metrics.

#### Overview

Zendesk is a customer support platform used to manage tickets, requests, and related customer service workflows.

#### What this integration powers:

- Reporting on support volume and throughput, including ticket creation, updates, and resolution trends
- Visibility into operational workload by queue, priority, or request type to help quantify support burden
- Performance tracking for support workflows, such as response and resolution patterns over time
