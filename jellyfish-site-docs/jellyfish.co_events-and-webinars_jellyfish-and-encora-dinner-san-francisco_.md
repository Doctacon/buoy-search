# Data-Driven Transformation: Elevating Engineering Management from Optional to Essential

August 28th, 5:30pm- 8:30pm PST

La Mar \| Pier 1 1/2 The Embarcadero, San Francisco, CA 94111

Historically, anecdotes in software engineering management were acceptable, as they were often the only option. If you’re thinking, “Hey, anecdotes don’t cut it anymore” you’re not alone.

Together, Jellyfish & [Encora](https://www.encora.com/) provide data-informed solutions to fuel process improvements for engineering leaders and teams that go beyond anecdotes and drive better business results.

Join us on Wednesday, August 28th for an executive R&D dinner hosted by Jellyfish and Encora at La Mar, best known for its Peruvian plates. The evening promises networking opportunities and insightful discussions on evolving approaches and challenges in leveraging data for impactful results in software engineering management.

Special guest Kevin Isacks, VP of Cloud, Media, and Video Engineering at Avid Technologies will join us and share his story, strategies, and insights gained from years of leading engineering teams.

We’d love to have you, please reserve your spot soon as seating is limited. If you have any questions, please contact [events@jellyfish.co](mailto:events@jellyfish.co)
