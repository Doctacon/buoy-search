**_Recognized for Completeness of Vision and Ability to Execute_**

We are excited to share that Jellyfish has been recognized as a Leader in the 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Developer Productivity Insight Platforms.

We believe this recognition arrives at a critical inflection point: the rise of Agentic AI. As an increasing share of code is written by autonomous agents and agents expand to other parts of the SDLC, the very definition of “productivity” is being rewritten. Jellyfish is uniquely engineered for this new reality, providing the intelligence R&D teams need to orchestrate a hybrid workforce of humans and AI agents. By surfacing deep insights into how these autonomous tools impact velocity and quality, Jellyfish enables R&D teams to become truly AI-native, turning the speed of autonomous agents into measurable strategic outcomes.

**About Gartner and the Magic Quadrant**

Gartner delivers actionable, objective insight to executives and their teams. Its expert guidance and tools enable faster, smarter decisions and stronger performance on an organization’s mission-critical priorities. The Gartner Magic Quadrant evaluates vendors based on their Ability to Execute and Completeness of Vision. We are honored to be included among the recognized vendors in this important report. Learn more about the Magic Quadrant.
