## R&D doesn’t have to be a black box. Demystify R&D spend with Jellyfish.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/RD-black-boxv2-2.webp)

As companies scale past their initial stages of growth to ~$100M+ in revenue, they tend to spend ~30% of their annual top-line on R&D, with 75-80% of that spend going towards headcount. Yet, R&D widely remains a mystery.

- What are R&D teams working on?
- Are they focused on business critical projects?
- How do you evaluate performance and efficiency in an objective manner?
