# How to Build a United Front between Product and Engineering

###### A Fireside Chat with Andrew Lau, CEO of Jellyfish and Srinivas Krishnamurti, SVP Product at Productboard

We all know that fostering that alignment between Product and Engineering isn’t always the easiest task. Product and Engineering leaders have the same goals – to build and deliver successful products – and yet they are often incentivized differently and approach problems from different angles. At the end of the day, building great products and creating business value depends on good alignment and collaboration across these two teams.

Listen to Andrew Lau, CEO of Jellyfish and Srinivas Krishnamurti, SVP Product discuss how we can drive better alignment and ultimately a stronger relationship between Product and Engineering, and why that’s so important to the success of your business.
