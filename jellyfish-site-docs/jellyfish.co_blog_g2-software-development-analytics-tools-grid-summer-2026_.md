## How G2 Scores Are Calculated

G2’s rankings are highly respected because they are rooted in authentic, real-time, and unbiased peer reviews. Products are categorized using a standardized methodology, and scores are determined by a mix of public online signals and verified user review data, specifically looking at:

- G2’s Satisfaction algorithms
- User satisfaction
- Review quality
- Product growth (employee, web, and social)
- Year-over-year trends
