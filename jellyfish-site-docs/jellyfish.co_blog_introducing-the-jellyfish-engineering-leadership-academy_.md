December 19, 2024

At Jellyfish, we believe exceptional engineering teams are built on strong leadership. That’s why we’re thrilled to announce the [Engineering Leadership Academy](https://jellyfish.co/learn/engineering-leadership-academy/), created in partnership with some of the most respected engineering practitioners in the industry.

The Engineering Leadership Academy is designed to equip current and aspiring engineering leaders with the practical skills, proven strategies, and leadership principles they need to build and lead world-class teams.

## What You’ll Learn

The Academy features a robust course catalog led by industry experts who bring real-world experience to every lesson. Here’s a preview of what you’ll learn:

- **Fundamentals of Engineering Leadership** _with_ [_Tacita Morway_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tacita/) _, CTO at Textio_
Gain foundational skills in engineering management, including how to translate technical work for non-technical audiences, present to boards, navigate management theory, and make the leap from individual contributor to leader.

- **Understanding Organizational Design** _with_ [_Andrew Korbel_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrew-korbel-leader/) _, VP R&D Operations and Chief of Staff at Clari_
Discover strategies for assessing and optimizing organizational structure, aligning design with strategic goals, and fostering a culture of continuous, rapid improvement.

- **Building & Managing High-Performing Teams** _with_ [_Wes Whitlam_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/weswhitlam/) _, Director, Platform Automation & Developer Experience at Rockwell Automation_
Learn how to build dynamic teams – including managing remote and hybrid teams – hire effectively, foster innovation, and ensure consistent high performance.

- **Metrics & Performance Analysis in Engineering** _with_ [_Eli Daniel_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/eli-daniel/) _, Head of Engineering &_ [_Luke Stevens_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luke-stevens-875a295/) _, Director of Engineering at Jellyfish_
Develop an effective metrics strategy to identify and analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) that drive results while maintaining psychological safety for your teams.

- **Culture, Inclusion & Ethics in Engineering Leadership** _with_ [_Colleen Tartow_](https://www.linkedin.com/in/colleen-tartow-phd/) _, Field CTO & Head of Strategy at Vast Data_
Explore actionable approaches to embedding culture, inclusion, and ethical practices into your leadership style.
