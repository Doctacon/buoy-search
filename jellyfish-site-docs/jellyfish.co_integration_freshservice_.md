# Freshservice Integration

Sync Freshservice service requests tickets to track operational workload and performance metrics.

#### Overview

Freshservice is an IT service management platform used to manage service desk tickets, incidents, and support workflows.

#### What this integration powers:

- Visibility into service desk workload by tracking ticket volume, status changes, and priority shifts over time
- Operational performance reporting using Freshservice ticket and incident activity signals across teams and time periods
- Better context on interruptions by analyzing support ticket activity alongside delivery metrics in Jellyfish
