## Frequently Asked Questions

What is engineering resource allocation, and why does it matter?

Engineering resource allocation is how teams distribute time and effort across projects, initiatives, and investment categories. Accurate allocations help leaders optimize budgets, reduce waste, and ensure engineering effort aligns with business priorities.

How does Jellyfish help engineering teams improve business alignment?

Jellyfish analyzes real engineering work—across Jira, ADO, Linear, SCM systems, and more—to show where time is spent, how work maps to goals, and where misalignment or bottlenecks exist. Leaders get a clear view of investment trends and progress toward strategic outcomes.

What makes Jellyfish’s allocation model different from manual time tracking?

Unlike manual reporting or spreadsheets, Jellyfish uses a patented, multi-source data model to categorize work automatically. This provides accurate, audit-ready allocations without requiring time tracking, data cleanup, or workflow changes.

Who benefits most from Jellyfish’s Business Alignment insights?

Engineering leaders, EMs, PMs, Finance, and Operations teams use Jellyfish to understand work distribution, track investment against targets, and make informed decisions about priorities, staffing, and strategic direction.

Can Jellyfish work with incomplete or messy data?

Yes. Jellyfish’s patented model is built to handle real-world data quality. It unifies signals from multiple sources and automatically classifies work, providing reliable allocation insights—even when your issue tracking hygiene isn’t perfect.
