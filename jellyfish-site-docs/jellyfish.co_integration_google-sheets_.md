# Google Sheets Integration

Connect Sheets to keep your Roster current keeping people, cost, and allocation data accurate.

#### Overview

Google Sheets lets you manage roster data in a spreadsheet and apply it to Jellyfish either as a one-time import or an automated nightly refresh. This keeps Person records consistent and helps ensure downstream reporting reflects the right people context.

#### What this integration powers:

- Cleaner People, Teams, and org context by keeping role, level, location, and manager fields up to date
- More accurate Allocations and DevFinOps outputs by maintaining cost, allocation fraction, and engineer classification fields
- Better continuity in historical reporting by updating roster records without disrupting past work attribution
