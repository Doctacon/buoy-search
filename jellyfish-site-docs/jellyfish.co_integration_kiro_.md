# Kiro Integration

Evaluate Kiro adoption and usage to quantify the productivity impact of AI-assisted development.

#### Overview

Kiro is an agentic AI IDE that uses spec-driven development to help engineers move from prototype to production with structure and speed.

#### What this integration powers:

- AI Impact insights on Kiro adoption and usage patterns by developer, team, and repo
- Engagement reporting including active usage days and adoption trends over time
- Data to measure AI development tooling ROI and support productivity investment decisions
