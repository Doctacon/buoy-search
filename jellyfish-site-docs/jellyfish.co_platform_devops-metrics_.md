## Continuous improvement with DORA

According to research conducted by the DORA group, software teams that achieve the highest levels of performance, delivering value quickly and consistently, prioritize four important metrics. By using Jellyfish, teams can track these metrics or adjust DORA metrics to align with their objectives.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Devopsmetrics-deployment-frequency-IMG-1.webp)

#### Deployment frequency

Track how often the team pushes deployments. See the impact of process and tooling changes to ensure continuous improvement.

#### Lead time for changes

Understand the duration of your full value delivery cycle, from the moment a change is selected for development to its deployment.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Devopsmetrics-lead-time-changes-IMG-1.webp)

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Devopsmetrics-mean-time-resolution-IMG-1.webp)

#### Mean time to resolution

Measure the time from the start of an incident to its resolution, and minimize system downtime.

#### Change failure rate

Monitor the frequency of incidents or deployment failures to guarantee uninterrupted delivery of value to your customers.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Devopsmetrics-change-failure-rate-IMG-1.webp)
