# Jellyfish Named a Leader by G2

For the eighth consecutive quarter, Jellyfish has been recognized as a Leader within the Software Development Analytics Tools Grid by G2, the world’s largest and most trusted software marketplace.

We outperformed our competition in every area…and for a good reason: our customers’ overwhelmingly positive reviews and ratings!

The G2 grid report shows how Jellyfish stacks up to competitors based on:

- Ease of doing business
- Satisfaction with the product’s quality of support
- Likelihood to recommend each product
- Number of reviews

Download the grid report today and find out what users like best (and least) about Software Development Analytics software.

As you evaluate Engineering Management Platforms and Software Development Analytics tools, how do you know which provider to trust to give you a holistic view of your engineering organization?

That’s where 3rd party reports like this Summer 2024 Grid report comes in.

Inside, you’ll learn about software and services, view aggregated review scores for each technology, and compare solutions to find the best fit for your engineering team. You can use this G2 Grid report to:

– Identify the right tools for your business needs

– Inform your business case for software purchasing

– Get insights into market trends
