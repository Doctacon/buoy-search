We’ve got exciting news brewing! Our next Engineering Leader Coffee Chat is happening on **Friday, December 13th** from 12-1 PM EST.

Coffee Chats are casual discussions with fellow software engineering leaders dishing out experiences, challenges, and best practices – no Jellyfish talk allowed!

This month our spicy discussion topic is **AI Festivus: An Airing of Grievances**. Let’s take a walk down memory lane and relive 2024 AI hits and misses. We’ll also discuss R&D predictions for 2025 relating to all things new technologies/AI. What will **actually** change the way we develop products? Tricia Gregg, Jellyfish Product Manager and AI strategist, will join us to share her thoughts on what’s to come.

Bring your hot takes, your hot cup of coffee (or egg nog), and get ready for a great discussion with like-minded engineering leaders.

We hope to see you there!
