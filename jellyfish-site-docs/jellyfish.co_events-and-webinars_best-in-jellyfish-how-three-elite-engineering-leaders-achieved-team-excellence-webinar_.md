# Best in Jellyfish: How Three Elite Engineering Leaders Achieved Team Excellence

What do elite engineering teams have in common, and how do they achieve success? After an incredibly intensive selection process, we are happy to announce the winners of Illuminate 2022, Jellyfish’s first-ever software engineering leadership awards program. Watch this live panel discussion to learn more about how Submittable, Hootsuite, and Loadsmart are advancing innovation in software engineering, and why they were selected as winners.

On December 6, at 1pm ET, we featured our winners and discussed how these engineering teams demonstrated consistent commitment to DevOps principles and achieved excellence across DORA metrics with Jellyfish.

**Hear from Submittable, Hootsuite, and Loadsmart as they discuss:**

- Components of  great engineering leadership
- How they defined and achieved excellence
- How they distinguished themselves between growth, innovation and feature work
