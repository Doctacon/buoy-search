# Google Calendar Integration

Pull meeting data to quantify meeting load and focus trends.

#### Overview

Google Calendar is where your teams schedule meetings and protect focus time. Connecting Google Calendar to Jellyfish helps you understand meeting effort for engineers across teams, groups, and the full org, without relying on manual reporting.

#### What this integration powers:

- Meeting effort insights: Quantify meeting load for engineers by team, group, division, and company.
- Focus and load trends: Spot patterns in meeting volume and duration over time to protect focus time.
- Meeting hygiene visibility: Filter out non-meetings (OOO, focus/flow time, appointments) so the signal is cleaner.
