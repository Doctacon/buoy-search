Even amid a heavy emphasis on efficiency, engineering teams are still growing in order to support product delivery, with 67% of engineering organizations seeing increased budgets this year. Yet according to the 2024 State of Engineering Management Report, 65% experienced burnout in the past year. The problem was particularly acute for short-staffed engineers and leaders overseeing large organizations.

Watch Genius Sports CTO, Michale Patel, and West Monroe Partner, Mike Miner, discuss with Jellyfish cofounder, Phil Braden, the insights from the 2024 State of Engineering Management Report and how you can balance rebuilding your engineering team while safeguarding against burnout.

#### Takeaways include:

The long term impacts of burnout on productivity

Hiring challenges by company size

Strategies for growth and efficiency to support product delivery and innovation
