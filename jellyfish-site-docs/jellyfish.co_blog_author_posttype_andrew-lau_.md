# Andrew Lau

Andrew Lau is Co-Founder and CEO of [Jellyfish](https://jellyfish.co/), the leading Software Engineering Intelligence Platform that enables engineering, finance and business leaders to build more effective R&D organizations by uncovering actionable insights on team health and performance, investment allocations, and delivery. Jellyfish’s market-leading software is used by more than 500 companies globally.

Andrew lives in Cambridge, MA with his wife and two children. He holds a degree in Computer Science from MIT.
