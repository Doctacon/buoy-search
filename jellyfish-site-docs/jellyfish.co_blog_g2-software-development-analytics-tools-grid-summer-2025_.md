We’re equally excited to be a leader in the Momentum Grid Report for Software Development Analytics Tools for the ninth quarter in a row, officially taking the **#1 leader spot** this quarter with a score of 86 out of 100. G2’s Momentum Grid® ranks products using a combination of user review data and public online signals. Scores are calculated based on:

- G2’s Satisfaction and Momentum algorithms
- User satisfaction
- Review quality
- Product growth (employee, web, and social)
- Year-over-year trends.

All products are categorized through G2’s standardized methodology, and ratings are unbiased, real-time, and verified for authenticity.
