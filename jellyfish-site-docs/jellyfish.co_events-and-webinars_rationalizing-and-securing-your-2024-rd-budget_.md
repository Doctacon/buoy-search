# Rationalizing and Securing your 2024 R&D Budget

Between justifying costs, aligning on expectations, and planning your roadmap for 2024, the annual budgeting process can create headaches for engineering & finance leaders alike.

Learn how to justify your engineering budget, while establishing harmony between finance, the wider business and the board. We’ll cover:

- Strategies for securing alignment between engineering and the business
- How to justify and defend your budget in a precarious economic landscape
- How Jellyfish can help you make meaningful budget planning decisions
