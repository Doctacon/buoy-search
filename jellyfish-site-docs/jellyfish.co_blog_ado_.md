Today we are excited to announce that Jellyfish now integrates with [Microsoft Azure DevOps](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/devops) (ADO), giving joint customers the ability to see work items, project, and team information alongside key engineering productivity metrics directly in the Jellyfish platform.

![Jellyfish ADO Integration](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/Jellyfish-ADO-Integration.png)

This new integration gives engineering leaders using ADO better visibility into how work is allocated, whether teams are focused on priority projects, the ability to more proactively identify bottlenecks, how their teams are adopting AI and its overall impact, and much more.

Engineering organizations standardizing their tech stacks around Microsoft tools can now readily see their ADO Board data in Jellyfish to gain a truly complete picture of their organization’s engineering work, bringing us one step closer to our goal of serving as the single source of truth for all work happening within the R&D organization.

Key Jellyfish ADO capabilities include:

- Visibility into work allocation across teams and individuals to spot bottlenecks and areas for improvement
- Ability to visualize how much investment is going to different engineering projects, enabling leaders to understand whether teams are aligned to business priorities
- Dashboards that surface trends and key insights so team leaders can take action and properly report performance to executive stakeholders
- Key engineering productivity metrics such as DORA, and delivery forecasts to uncover bottlenecks and course-correct if needed
- AI-driven categorization of work, allowing even companies with “messy data” to access accurate data analysis and insights without manual effort
- Access to Jellyfish AI Impact for full visibility into how AI tools are driving changes across the SDLC, and much more.
