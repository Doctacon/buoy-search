# Everything You Need to Scale AI Effectively

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-User-analytics.webp)

### Cohort Intelligence

Group users into power, active, and casual cohorts to understand depth of usage and where behaviors differ.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-recommendations.webp)

### Adaptive Enablement Plans

Get tailored suggestions on how to move each user group up the curve using proven workflows and real-world patterns.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-enabled-users-Drill-down-filters.webp)

### Precision Filtering

Drill into usage by role, team, manager, or geography to tailor rollout strategies to specific segments of your org.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-AI-enablement-score.webp)

### AI Enablement Score

Combine satisfaction, confidence, and friction signals to understand onboarding gaps and guide smoother adoption.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-AI-usage-dates.webp)

### Rollout Clarity

Track usage over time to see which rollout moments sparked lift, where progress stalled, and how to course-correct.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-filter-highlight.webp)

### Tool-Level Usage Views

Toggle between aggregate usage and individual assistants or agents to pinpoint where certain AI tools need extra support.
