## Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevFinOps and why does it matter for software capitalization?

DevFinOps brings engineering and finance data together to automate software capitalization and R&D cost reporting. It gives finance teams accurate, audit-ready insights into engineering effort without time tracking or manual spreadsheets.

How does Jellyfish automate software capitalization reporting?

Jellyfish calculates engineering effort automatically using signals from Jira, ADO, GitHub, and other tools, layers in roster and cost data, and generates audit-ready reports that determine what work is capitalizable.

Can Jellyfish help with R&D tax credits and financial audits?

Yes. Jellyfish provides precise, defensible effort calculations and categorization of R&D activities, making it easier to document eligible work, support audits, and claim tax credit programs accurately.

What data does Jellyfish use to calculate engineering effort and cost?

Jellyfish pulls signals from your engineering systems—tickets, commits, reviews, and workflows—and combines them with roster, payroll, and cost data to determine engineering effort, cost allocations, and capitalizable work.

How does DevFinOps improve collaboration between engineering and finance?

DevFinOps standardizes the language, processes, and reports used by engineering and finance, eliminating manual back-and-forth, reducing errors, and giving both teams shared visibility into development spend and investment alignment.
