We’ve got exciting news brewing. Our next Engineering Leader Coffee Chat is happening on **Friday, January 17th** from 12-1 PM EST.

Coffee Chats are casual discussions with fellow software engineering leaders serving up experiences, challenges, and best practices – no Jellyfish talk allowed!

This month our spicy discussion topic is **The Future of R&D Organizations: A Race to Infinite Productivity**. We’ll be exploring how AI is reshaping the roles of product managers, engineers, and the organizations that support them.

We’ll discuss things like:

- How will Product Manager/Designers adapt to focus on strategy and innovation?
- Will “the hot new programming language” be English?
- How should bootcamps, CS programs, and team dynamics evolve to prepare junior engineers and adapt to AI-driven workflows?

Bring your hot takes, your hot cup of coffee, and get ready for a great discussion with engineering leaders like you for our first coffee chat of the year!
