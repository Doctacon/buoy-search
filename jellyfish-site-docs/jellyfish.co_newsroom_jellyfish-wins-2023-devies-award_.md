### **Engineering Management Platform recognized at DeveloperWeek 2022 in the Enterprise Development & Integration Solutions category.**

**BOSTON, February** **15, 2** **023** – [Jellyfish](https://www.jellyfish.co/), the leading Engineering Management Platform, today announced that the company’s platform won a [2023 DEVIES Award](https://www.developerweek.com/awards/) in the enterprise development and integration solutions category. The awards recognize outstanding design, engineering, and innovation in developer technology.

Jellyfish will receive its award during [DeveloperWeek 2023](https://www.developerweek.com/), the world’s largest developer conference with more than 8,000 participants from over 150 countries across the globe. The DEVIES Award winners, selected from a record-high 310 nominations, were judged on the attention attracted in the software industry, general regard and use by the developer community, and technical leadership for innovation.

“The Jellyfish team is building a platform that customers love and is helping enterprises achieve their engineering goals,” said Andrew Lau, CEO at Jellyfish. “This recognition further validates our innovative software and commitment to maximizing the business impact of engineering as we advance our mission.”

The Jellyfish engineering management platform provides modern engineering leaders the visibility and actionable insights they need to manage teams, make data-driven decisions and optimize engineering operations. Jellyfish recently bolstered its platform with the launch of a [Benchmarking tool](https://www.jellyfish.co/engineering-benchmarks/), [DevFinOps solution](https://www.jellyfish.co/platform/devfinops/), and [Life Cycle Explorer](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-life-cycle-explorer-announcement/), which lets teams identify and resolve the root cause of bottlenecks in the software development process.

“Developer tools and technology product solutions are leading the way for software developers & engineers to build upon the foundation of the ever-expanding technology sector. Jellyfish’s win is evidence of its leading role in the growth and innovation in the software industry,” said Jonathan Pasky, executive producer of DevNetwork, producer of DeveloperWeek and the 2023 DEVIES Awards.

Jellyfish was recently named as one of Built In’s [2023 Best Places to Work](https://builtin.com/awards/boston/2023/best-places-to-work) and listed among SD Times’ companies to watch in 2023. The company also earned a spot on the [Intelligent Apps 40 list](https://www.ia40.com/the-list) presented by Madrona, Goldman Sachs, PitchBook, Microsoft and Amazon.

To learn more about the Jellyfish platform, visit [https://www.jellyfish.co/](https://www.jellyfish.co/).
