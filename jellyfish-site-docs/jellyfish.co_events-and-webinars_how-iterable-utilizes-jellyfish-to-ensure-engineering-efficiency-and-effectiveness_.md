# How Iterable Utilizes Jellyfish To Ensure Engineering Efficiency and Effectiveness

Optimizing engineering operations sounds simple in theory but is exigent in practice. Engineering leaders are expected to implement initiatives to maximize efficiency across their organizations, but what initiatives should be prioritized for the best outcomes and what does that process look like?

Hear from Jeff Sing, Director of Engineering at Iterable, for a look into his strategy for proactively improving engineering efficiency, alignment, and impact.

This session covered:

- How to determine what’s important for your engineering organization
- How to align optimization initiatives to OKRs
- Tools to leverage to help drive impact
