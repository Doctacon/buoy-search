# 10 KPIs Every Engineering Leader Should Track

Data-driven decision making is critical for Engineering leadership today. But exactly which data should technical leaders pay attention to? What metrics should they be tracking as your team’s key performance indicators (KPIs)?

This e-book will walk you through the 10 KPIs that can help you answer these questions, and if you can track all 10 of these, you’ll be well on your way with the data you need to provide continuous value to your customers and your company.
