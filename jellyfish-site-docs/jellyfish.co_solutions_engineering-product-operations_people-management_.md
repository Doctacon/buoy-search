## People insights, grounded in real work

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-individual-summary.webp)

### See the full individual picture

Understand individual performance through a complete view of goals, investments, and recent work, so coaching is personalized and effective.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-activity-recap.webp)

### Prepare for better 1:1s

Get a concise summary of an individual’s week, including wins and potential challenges, to guide more meaningful 1:1 conversations.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-discussion-topics.webp)

### Keep conversations aligned

Capture notes, decisions, and follow-ups alongside the work itself, creating continuity and shared understanding over time.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-jf-assistant-1-1.webp)

### Get answers without digging

Ask Jellyfish Assistant for deeper insight and get instant, thorough answers grounded in your organization’s full people and work data.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-devex-comments.webp)

### Respond to team needs

Collect direct, actionable feedback through tailored developer surveys to uncover bottlenecks, morale issues, and opportunities to improve developer experience.
