## Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jellyfish Planning & Delivery?

It’s a unified system that helps engineering leaders plan realistically, forecast delivery, and manage risk using actual performance data.

How does Jellyfish improve delivery predictability?

By connecting planning and execution data, Jellyfish automatically flags risks, adjusts forecasts, and helps leaders course-correct before deadlines slip.

Can Jellyfish predict delivery risks before they happen?

Yes. Jellyfish analyzes capacity, throughput, and scope changes to alert you early when a project is off track, and gives you the tools to adjust before deadlines slip.

Can Jellyfish model different delivery scenarios?

Yes. Scenario Planner lets you test trade-offs in resources, scope, and priorities so you can make data-driven decisions when projects go off track.

How is Jellyfish different from project management tools?

While PM tools track tasks, Jellyfish connects planning, delivery, and historical performance data to show why projects succeed or fall behind, and what to do next.

Does Jellyfish work with my current planning tools?

Yes. Jellyfish integrates with Jira, ADO, Linear issue tracking systems, and GitHub, GitLab, Git, BitBucket, and ADO Repos to create a single, accurate view of delivery performance.

How long does it take to see results?

Teams typically see improved forecast accuracy and project visibility within weeks of implementation, without needing to change their existing workflows. Our dedicated implementation and support team is here to ensure you see results.
