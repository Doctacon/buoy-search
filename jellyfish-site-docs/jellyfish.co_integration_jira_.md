# Jira Integration

Sync epics, issues, and statuses to track delivery and investment allocation.

#### Overview

Jira is an issue tracking and project management platform used to plan, track, and manage software work. Jellyfish supports Jira Cloud and Jira Data Center via direct connection or the Jellyfish Agent. Jellyfish captures metadata about assigned ticket movements and ticket management events, including out-of-the-box and custom fields.

#### What this integration powers:

- Allocations and investment views based on ticket movement and work distribution
- Delivery visibility, including team summaries, deliverables, and workflow analysis in Life Cycle Explorer
- Engineering and operational metrics, including DevOps lead time metrics and broader performance reporting
