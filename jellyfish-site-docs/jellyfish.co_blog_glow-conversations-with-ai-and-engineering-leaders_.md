## Why GLOW?

At Jellyfish, our mission has always been to help engineering organizations make better decisions through data, insights, and understanding. As AI becomes embedded across the software development lifecycle, leaders need more than predictions – they need practical perspectives from peers navigating these changes in real time.

GLOW was created to bring those perspectives together.

Through in-depth conversations with engineering executives, product leaders, AI innovators, researchers, and technology pioneers, GLOW explores how organizations are adopting AI, what challenges they’re encountering, and how they’re preparing for the future of software development.

The goal isn’t to provide definitive answers. It’s to ask better questions.

## Exploring the Questions That Matter Most

Across the series, we’ll dive into four themes that are shaping the future of engineering:

- **AI in the SDLC:** From prototyping and coding to testing and code review, AI is fundamentally reshaping engineering workflows. Our guests share what they’re seeing inside their organizations, where AI is delivering value today, and where human expertise remains irreplaceable.
- **Tooling & Adoption:** Engineering leaders are tasked with evaluating, selecting, and scaling new technologies while balancing security, governance, and developer experience. GLOW explores the lessons learned from teams on the front lines of AI adoption.
- **Measurement & Impact:** Beyond anecdotes and hype, organizations need ways to measure productivity gains, engineering effectiveness, and business outcomes. Our conversations examine how leading teams are approaching measurement and defining success in an AI-driven world.
- **The Future of Engineering:** As AI capabilities accelerate, traditional assumptions about roles, team structures, hiring, and organizational design are being challenged. GLOW brings together bold perspectives on where the industry may be headed next.
