# Deep Dive \| What’s next for engineering in the age of AI?

Reflections on 2025 and Predictions for 2026

2025 has been a defining year for software development. From the acceleration of AI-assisted coding to the rise of data-driven engineering management, teams have been forced to adapt faster than ever.

As we close out the year, Jellyfish advisor Adam Ferrari and special guest Melanie Ziegler, CEO of VPE Forum, will reflect on where we’ve come from and look ahead to what’s next for the software development lifecycle in 2026.

Together, they’ll discuss:

- How AI transformed engineering in 2025: What’s changed, and what’s just hype
- Predictions for 2026: Where AI, automation, and developer productivity are headed next
- The evolving role of engineering leaders and how to guide teams through another year of rapid innovation

This is your chance to hear firsthand from two of the leading voices shaping the future of engineering. Save your spot today to get ahead of what’s next in software development and prepare for 2026.
