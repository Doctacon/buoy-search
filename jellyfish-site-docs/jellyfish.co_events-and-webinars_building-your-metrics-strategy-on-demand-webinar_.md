# Building Your Metrics Strategy

Metrics are selected to be quantitative proxies for performance and vary widely in ease of use and value to an organization. Regardless of what you measure, the system will cheat, so it’s important to have a solid metrics strategy in place.

Hear from Milan Thakker, Product Manager at Jellyfish, on how you can optimize your metrics strategy with Jellyfish.

###### Watch now to learn:

- How to focus on metrics outcomes over outputs
- Why grouping metrics according to team outcomes is important
- How to optimize DevOps outcomes
