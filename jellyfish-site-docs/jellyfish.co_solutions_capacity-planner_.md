#### Accurately forecast capacity

Predict workload capacity accurately by accounting for everything in motion – historical allocation data, ongoing projects, planned work, upcoming priorities, and team size.

#### Adapt to shifting priorities smoothly

Make dynamic adjustments by refining project scopes, reallocating resources, and recalibrating delivery plans as priorities change – maintaining project momentum without overloading teams.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/capacity-planner-shifting-priorities.webp)

#### Avoid team burnout with achievable workloads

Aligning delivery goals with accurate capacity forecasts ensures manageable workloads, enabling teams to meet deadlines and deliver quality work without sacrificing morale.
