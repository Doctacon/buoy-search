For the 10th consecutive quarter, Jellyfish has maintained its position as the Leader in [G2’s Software Development Analytics Tools Grid](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools?utf8=%E2%9C%93&selected_view=grid#grid). This milestone reflects not just our consistency but our dedication to delivering the best tools for engineering teams everywhere.

In the Winter 2025 G2 Grid Report, we’re honored to remain the top-ranked platform in user Satisfaction Ratings with a score of 98 out of 100. It’s a reflection of our mission to always put customers first and create solutions that truly make an impact.

We’re equally excited to lead the Mid-Market Relationship Index, scoring 9.43 out of 10. This score reflects our strengths in key areas including:

- **Ease of Doing Business:** Jellyfish makes collaboration simple and efficient.
- **Quality of Support:** Satisfaction with the quality of our customer support
- **Likelihood to Recommend:** 90% of users say they’d recommend Jellyfish.
- **Number of Reviews:** More reviews mean greater trust and indicate a more representative and accurate reflection of the customer experience

## At the Heart of It All: User Satisfaction

While this recognition is exciting, what drives us is the satisfaction of our customers. Their feedback shows the real impact Jellyfish has on engineering organizations:

- 97% of users gave Jellyfish a 4- or 5-star rating
- 94% believe we’re moving in the right direction
- 90% would recommend Jellyfish to others

These numbers remind us why we do what we do – help engineering teams work smarter, stay aligned, and achieve meaningful results.
