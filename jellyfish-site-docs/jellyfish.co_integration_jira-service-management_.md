# Jira Service Management Integration

Sync Jira Service Management service desk tickets and change management data to track workload and performance metrics.

#### Overview

Jira Service Management is an ITSM platform used to manage service requests, incidents, and operational changes through a service desk workflow.

#### What this integration powers:

- Visibility into service desk workload by tracking ticket volume, status movement, and activity over time
- Operational performance reporting using issue, comment, and worklog signals tied to support workflows
- Better end-to-end context by analyzing operational interruptions alongside engineering delivery signals in Jellyfish
