We’re thrilled to announce that for the eighth consecutive quarter, Jellyfish has been named a Leader in [G2’s Software Development Analytics Tools Grid](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools#grid). Not only have we secured the top spot, but we have also earned the highest [user satisfaction rating](https://www.g2.com/reports/grid-report-for-software-development-analytics-tools-summer-2024.embed?secure%5Bgated_consumer%5D=e8821105-7d95-46c0-a5f1-40682fdcf65c&secure%5Btoken%5D=d02168a99eacca7e7a4adf106dde097ddc517d55c35014f2b8757feb2492b22a&tab=satisfaction) among our competitors.

Jellyfish has also maintained a leader role within the Mid-Market Relationship Index for Software Development Analytics Tools, achieving a score of 9.48/10, the highest among competitors. The relationship index is calculated using four factors:

- **Ease of Doing Business:** Customers’ reported ease of doing business with the seller based on reviews by G2 users
- **Quality of Support:** Customers’ satisfaction with the product’s quality of support based on reviews by G2 users
- **Likelihood to Recommend:** Customers’ likelihood to recommend each product based on reviews by G2 users
- **Number of Reviews:** Buyers trust a product with more reviews, and a greater number of reviews indicates a more representative and accurate reflection of the customer experience.

These recognitions are a testament to the value and effectiveness of our platform and the trust and approval of our users. Download a copy of the [Summer 2024 G2 Grid Report.](https://www.jellyfish.co/resources/g2-grid-report-summer-2024/)
