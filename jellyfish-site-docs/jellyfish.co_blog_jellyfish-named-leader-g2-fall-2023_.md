September 12, 2023

As Fall 2023 begins, we have some exciting news to share.

For the fifth consecutive quarter, the Jellyfish Engineering Management Platform has been recognized as a Leader within [the Software Development Analytics Tools Grid](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools#grid) by [G2](https://www.g2.com/), the world’s largest and most trusted software marketplace.

We earned the recognition by receiving positive reviews from verified users compared to similar products in the same category. For inclusion in the report, a product must have received 10 or more reviews. Holding a Leader position for five reports in a row is no small feat, as you must be receiving new positive reviews on a regular basis to stay ahead in the grid.

“Rankings on G2 reports are based on data provided to us by real software buyers,” said Sara Rossio, Chief Product Officer at G2. “Potential buyers know they can trust these insights when researching and selecting software because they’re rooted in vetted, verified, and authentic reviews.”

We’re also excited to share that Jellyfish has been ranked Best Mid-Market Relationship within Software Development Analytics Tools for Fall 2023. The Best Mid-Market Relationship product in the Relationship Index earned [the highest Relationship rating](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools) in its category.

Following the launch of [Jellyfish DevFinOps](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/announcing-jellyfish-devfinops/) and [Meeting Insights](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-meeting-insights/) in 2022, we launched Jellyfish [Life Cycle Explorer](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-life-cycle-explorer-announcement/) in Q1, and [Scenario Planning](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-scenario-planning/) in May, a dedicated resource allocation modeling tool for their organization which can accurately plan and predict the engineers required to deliver software consistently.

Learn more about what real users have to say (or leave a [review of Jellyfish](https://www.g2.com/products/jellyfish-2022-03-01/reviews)!) on G2. To learn more about Jellyfish, visit the [product tour](https://www.jellyfish.co/product-tour/) or [request a demo](https://www.jellyfish.co/request-demo/) today.

G2 is the world’s largest and most trusted software marketplace. Thousands of software and services companies of all sizes partner with G2 to build their reputation, manage their software spend, and grow their business – including Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoom, and Adobe.
