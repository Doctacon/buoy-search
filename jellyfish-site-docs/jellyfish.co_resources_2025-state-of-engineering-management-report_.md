# 2025 State of Engineering Management Report

We surveyed **640+ CTOs, Engineering Managers, and Engineers** for the sixth consecutive year to get their perspectives on the State of Engineering Management. Here are some of the key findings:

– 90% are embracing AI coding tools

– 25% increase in developer velocity and productivity via AI coding

– 42% said GitHub Copilot is the tool of choice

– 25% of the work will be handled by AI five years from now

– 61% said their engineering budget increased as a percentage of company revenue YoY
