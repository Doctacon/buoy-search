# Women in Technology Leadership

March is Women’s History Month, and in honor of celebrating the contributions of women in contemporary society, Jellyfish would like to acknowledge women who are trailblazers in tech leadership.

We heard from distinguished female leaders as they discussed the challenges and triumphs they’ve experienced in their careers and their professional journeys in tech.

They also discussed challenges women in the tech field face and how we can all work towards providing a more equitable and inclusive future.
