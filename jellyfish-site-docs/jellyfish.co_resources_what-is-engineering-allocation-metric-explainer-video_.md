# What is Engineering Allocation? \| Metric Explainer Video

Jellyfish’s keystone engineering metric can be used to understand the resource breakdown of your engineering organization. Allocation is a tool that provides engineering managers and engineering leaders a KPI to understand how engineering resources are being allocated to different workspaces such as product roadmap, infrastructure, unplanned and more.
