## Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Jellyfish DevEx designed for?

Platform Engineering, Developer Productivity, and Engineering Leadership teams who want to understand and improve how developers experience their work.

How does Jellyfish DevEx measure developer experience?

It combines survey-based feedback with system metrics (like DORA and SPACE) to identify friction points, workflow inefficiencies, and opportunities to improve productivity.

Can Jellyfish benchmark developer experience across teams or industry?

Yes. DevEx provides team-level and industry benchmarks, helping leaders compare performance, track trends, and scale what top-performing teams do best. Other platforms only benchmark at the industry level.

What makes Jellyfish DevEx different from surveys or productivity tools?

Unlike standalone surveys or metric dashboards, Jellyfish unifies qualitative and quantitative data, so you can see why developers struggle and how improvements impact delivery.

How does Jellyfish connect developer experience to business outcomes?

By correlating developer sentiment with delivery metrics, Jellyfish quantifies how DevEx improvements impact speed, quality, and business results.

How can I get started with Jellyfish DevEx?

Request a demo to see how Jellyfish helps uncover friction, quantify experience, and improve developer productivity—without changing your current tools.
