# The Evolving World Of Engineering Leadership

For too long, engineering has lacked the necessary tools for strategic decision-making to match their counterparts in Sales and Marketing. But as Mom & Pop operations start adopting POS systems and gyms begin hiring full-stack developers, one thing is clear: Engineering is the core of modern business.

Tune into this panel exploring the evolving world of Engineering leadership to hear how leaders are adapting to the increasingly complex challenges posed by modern software engineering.
