# Networking Event for Bay-Area R&D Leaders

Cocktails, Conversations & Community

October 28th, 5:30 pm – 9:00 pm PST

Barrio, San Francisco

Join us for AfterGLOW San Francisco – a networking event for R&D leaders at Barrio! Whether you’re coming straight from GitHub Universe or you’re a local leader in the Bay Area, this is your chance to connect over premium cocktails, an array of Mexican cuisine, and a mix of creative and classic games — from Cards Against Engineering and Lego building to arcade favorites like Donkey Kong and Pac-Man.

Space is limited, RSVPs are subject to approval.

For questions, please contact events@jellyfish.co.
