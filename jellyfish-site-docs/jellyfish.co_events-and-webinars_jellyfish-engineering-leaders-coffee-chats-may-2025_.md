We’ve got exciting news brewing. Our next Engineering Leader Coffee Chat is happening on **Friday, May 30th** from 12-1 PM EST. Coffee Chats are casual discussions with fellow software engineering leaders serving up experiences, challenges, and best practices – no Jellyfish talk allowed!

This month, we’re discussing **Growth Mindset in the Age of AI**. With new AI tools popping up on the scene weekly, R&D leaders need to hire developers who can balance experimentation and focus.

[Pat Jenakanandhini](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jenakap/), Chief Product Officer at Tripleseat, will join us to share his hot takes on:

- Why new hires need to be willing to experiment with new AI tools
- How leaders can drive AI adoption from the top
- How to balance continuous learning with the need for execution and results

Bring your steaming cup of joe and your hottest takes for a great discussion with fellow engineering leaders.
