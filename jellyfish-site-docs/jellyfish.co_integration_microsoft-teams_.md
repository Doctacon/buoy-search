# Microsoft Teams Integration

Send DevEx alerts and reminders to ensure maximum survey participation.

#### Overview

Microsoft Teams is where many engineering orgs coordinate day to day. Connecting Teams to Jellyfish helps you run Developer Experience surveys with less manual follow-up by reaching engineers in the tool they already use.

#### What this integration powers:

- Survey invitations in Teams: Prompt engineers to respond with a clear call to action, right in their workflow.
- Automated reminders: Follow up with non-responders to lift response rates without extra admin work.
- End-of-survey visibility: Notify survey owners as the close date approaches with participation context so they can take quick action.
- Faster feedback loops: Collect qualitative input consistently, then use it to prioritize DevEx improvements with confidence.
