For more than 10 years, the DORA report has shaped engineering leadership trends and metrics for an entire industry. Now, as a Gold sponsor, Jellyfish is bringing the report’s insights to you first in an exclusive webinar, featuring the report’s lead author, Nathen Harvey.

Join us on Thursday, October 30th at 1pm EDT for a live look at the [2025 DORA report](http://dora.dev/dora-report-2025/).

This is your opportunity to get ahead of the trends shaping the field of engineering with an in-depth view into DORA performance metrics along with insights into how individuals, teams and workflows are changing year to year – with a special emphasis on AI adoption and impact.

Report findings are based on responses from more than 5,000 technology professionals from around the world along with more than 100 hours of qualitative data.

This year’s report focuses on four key areas:

1. Artificial intelligence
   - AI adoption and use
   - Exploring AI’s relationship to key outcomes
   - NEW: DORA AI capabilities model
   - The AI mirror
2. Software delivery performance
3. Platform engineering
4. Value stream management
