# GenAI: Perception vs Reality

##### An analysis of the impact of AI coding tools on 20,000 engineers across 300 companies

In 2024, Jellyfish introduced AI Impact to measure the impact of AI coding tools like GitHub Copilot on engineers and their workflows. We’ve since gathered data from over 20,000 engineers at more than 300 companies, giving us a representative sample of how engineering organizations are using AI and the impact it’s having on production.

Our original research is available for download to help engineering and business leaders understand whether they’re getting adequate return on their AI investments and how to set their organizations up for success in the future.
