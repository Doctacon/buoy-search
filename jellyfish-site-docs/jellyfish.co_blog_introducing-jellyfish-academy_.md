## What is Jellyfish Academy?

Jellyfish Academy is our comprehensive learning hub, crafted with engineering teams in mind. It brings together knowledge from Jellyfish experts, proven best practices, and tailored learning paths to give you the flexibility to learn at your own pace or level up with targeted content.

With Jellyfish Academy, engineering teams have access to relevant content from product leadership and advanced technical skills to team management and engineering project alignment.

## Curated Learning Experiences and So Much More

In Jellyfish Academy you’ll find:

- **Tailored Learning Paths:** Courses and paths are designed to meet the specific needs of Engineering Leadership, Product Leaders, and more, helping guide professionals at all levels toward impactful learning experiences.
- **Comprehensive Content:** 50 lessons across 18 courses including short-form videos, webinars, and practical exercises covering topics like [delivery management](https://www.jellyfish.co/solutions/software-delivery-management/), sprint practices, [DevFinOps](https://www.jellyfish.co/platform/devfinops/), and cross-functional alignment, to name just a few.
- **Quizzes & Certificates:** Test your knowledge with end-of-course quizzes, and earn certificates, so you can showcase your progress and new skills to your organization and on LinkedIn.
- **Flexible & Accessible Learning:** Access the Academy anytime, anywhere. Whether you’re looking to squeeze in a quick lesson or work through a longer learning path, Jellyfish Academy makes it easy to learn at your own pace.

## Own Your Learning Journey

Jellyfish Academy is a versatile way to educate you throughout your Jellyfish journey. Whether you’re just starting out or you’re already a Jellyfish power user looking to expand your knowledge, Jellyfish Academy has you covered:

**Onboarding**

New users can dive into curated learning paths aligned to their immediate needs. This structured approach ensures you’re set up for success from day one. In fact, we’re integrating Jellyfish Academy into our onboarding process, making certain courses part of the standard journey for all new Jellyfish users.

**New Hires**

New users in your organization can ramp up quickly with on-demand lessons that walk them through key features and best practices. We know that training new hires can be time-consuming, but Jellyfish Academy helps streamline that process.

**Continuous Learning**

Jellyfish Academy isn’t just a one-time resource; it’s a long-term learning platform. With new courses and updated content regularly added, you can always find something new to learn. As you grow and want to explore more advanced features, the Academy is ready to guide you through the next phase of your journey with Jellyfish.

## Ready to get started? Access Jellyfish Academy Now.

Ready to get started? You can access Jellyfish Academy where it’s easiest for you – directly at [academy.jellyfish.co](http://academy.jellyfish.co/) with your Jellyfish SSO credentials or:

- In-app via your user profile dropdown
- Through [The Bloom](https://thebloom.jellyfish.co/), our online user community
- Via the [Jellyfish Help Center](https://help.jellyfish.co/hc/en-us) & Help Widget

We’re incredibly excited about what the Jellyfish Academy means for our users. This is a big step forward in empowering you to succeed with Jellyfish, fostering innovation, and encouraging continuous learning.
