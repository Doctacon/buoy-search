Chicago Executive Dinner

# The 5 Pillars of Software Engineering Management

June 12th, 5:30pm – 8:00pm CST

Prime & Provisions

As engineering leaders, the journey can often feel solitary, yet the challenges faced are shared.

Join us on June 12th for an exclusive dinner for Chicago-area executives looking to network, gain outside perspectives, and delve into the five pillars of a successful engineering organization. Hosted by Jellyfish, [West Monroe](https://www.westmonroe.com/) and [ELC](https://sfelc.com/) at the inspiring Prime & Provisions known for their incredible steak and views of the Chicago River, the dinner will focus on topics like:

- **Engineering Strategy & Business Alignment:** _How do you ensure your engineering strategy is closely aligned with your organization’s business objectives? _How do you know how your teams are performing vs. the industry and with the metrics that matter?__

- **Engineering & Product Operations:** _What strategies are you using to optimize engineering and product operations, enhance team collaboration, and drive efficiency?_

- **Software Delivery Management:** _How do you prioritize delivery to ensure predictability, quality, and speed while minimizing risks and bottlenecks?_

Space is limited, so be sure to reserve your seat as soon as possible. We look forward to hosting you for an evening of great food, conversation, and valuable connections. If you have any questions, please contact [events@jellyfish.co](mailto:events@jellyfish.co)
