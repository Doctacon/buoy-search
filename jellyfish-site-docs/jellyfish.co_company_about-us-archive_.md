# **Our story**

In today’s digital era, the role of Engineering goes beyond mere execution and becomes a driving force behind the business strategy. Our founders, Andrew, David, and Phil, experienced first-hand as R&D leaders how difficult it was to communicate the value and impact of engineering teams.

They realized we need to rethink the way we measure and communicate the impact of engineering teams; we need to elevate and empower engineering leaders to be business leaders; and we need to advance the way engineering teams operate, align to business needs, deliver value, and understand their roles.

Jellyfish was founded to do just that – so every engineering leader can be a business leader. Today, Jellyfish has freed up engineering leaders across the globe to focus on influencing product strategy and defining what’s next.
