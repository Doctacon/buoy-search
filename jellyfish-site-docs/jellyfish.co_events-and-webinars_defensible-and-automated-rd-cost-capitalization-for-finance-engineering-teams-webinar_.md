# Defensible and Automated R&D Cost Capitalization for Finance & Engineering Teams

For software engineering and finance leaders, capitalizing your organization’s software development costs can be immensely valuable (and sometimes mandated) for the business, effectively increasing profitability over time and potentially creating tax benefits. But R&D cost capitalization is often a complex manual, and inaccurate process.

How can finance and engineering leaders streamline the cumbersome process of R&D cost capitalization?

Watch this webinar to learn how new R&D cost capitalization tools in Jellyfish can dramatically improve your software capitalization efforts.

###### This webinar will cover how to:

- Easily report on the capital costs of product work and product development in a precise and auditable way to improve the bottom line.
- Automate and improve the reporting required for financial audits—without time tracking.
- Align development costs to revenue of specific products for better visibility into the real financial impact of the engineering team’s work.
