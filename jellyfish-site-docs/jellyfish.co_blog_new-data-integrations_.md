To lead a high-performing R&D organization, you need more than just code metrics. You need a holistic view of the entire software development lifecycle (SDLC). But too often critical data has been locked away in silos – from CI/CD workflows to Incident Management, Error Tracking and ITSM.

That’s why today we’re introducing more than 25 new [integrations](https://jellyfish.co/integrations/) to the Jellyfish platform, enabling you to connect the most critical tools across the SDLC to transform your fragmented toolchain into a cohesive intelligence engine.

This isn’t just about more connections – it’s about sharper context. By unifying these diverse data sources, engineering leaders can finally:

- **Eliminate Blind Spots:** See the full lifecycle of development, not just the commit history.
- **Uncover Correlations:** Understand exactly how incident volume impacts delivery speed or how code quality trends affect security.
- **Centralize Insights:** Stop trying to wrangle disparate data and focus on what matters with a single, unified view of engineering productivity.

Your Data, Your Way

## Your Data, Your Way

With the expanded ecosystem of data our new integrations provide, you need a way to slice and dice this information without limitations. Jellyfish’s new Query Builder (currently in Alpha) gives you the power to create custom queries for deeper insights and correlate data across disparate systems.

![Jellyfish Query Builder](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Jellyfish-Query-Builder.png)

With Query Builder, you can create, run and save SQL queries across any data pulled into Jellyfish – allowing you to answer even more nuanced questions.
