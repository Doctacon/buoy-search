Leadership isn’t just about keeping up – it’s about driving change. Today, we’re excited to highlight 35 remarkable leaders who are actively shaping the field of engineering and forging its path ahead.

These leaders, from CTOs to VPs, are pioneering innovations in fields like AI, cloud infrastructure, and data analytics. They’re mentors, visionaries, and trailblazers, leading the way for future generations of engineers while breaking down barriers in a traditionally male-dominated field. Keep reading to meet 35 amazing individuals leading the way in engineering:
