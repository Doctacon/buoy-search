How it works

## A Clear View of Team Performance

Jellyfish sits above your delivery tools to surface risks, capacity constraints, and workflow bottlenecks, turning daily activity into signals managers can act on.

![Jellyfish Business Alignment](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/business-alignment-product-hero.webp)

![Jellyfish Business Alignment](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/business-alignment-product-hero.webp)

## What Engineering Managers Can Do with Jellyfish

[Request a Demo](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/request-a-demo/)

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-metrics-explorer.webp)

### Measure Real Productivity

Understand how work flows so you can spot trends, tradeoffs, and improvement areas without vanity metrics.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-ai-assistant-context.webp)

### Get Answers Instantly

Ask team questions in plain language and get clear, contextual answers without digging through dashboards.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/feature-devexs-metrics-purple-bg.webp)

### Learn From Your Team

Easily capture team feedback to surface friction and prioritize improvements that actually help the team.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/feature-activity-recap.webp)

### Coach With Better Signals

Support 1:1s, feedback, and recognition with individual-level insights into work patterns, friction, and progress.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/feature-lifecycle-explorer.webp)

### Improve Team Flow

Trace work from intake to delivery to pinpoint bottlenecks, handoffs, and delays that slow your team down.

## Frequently Asked Questions

How does Jellyfish help engineering managers day to day?

Jellyfish gives engineering managers early signals on delivery risk, capacity, and focus. It reduces time spent chasing status updates and helps managers proactively unblock issues, coach engineers, and communicate clearly with stakeholders.

How does Jellyfish help with planning and predictability?

Jellyfish uses real delivery data to model capacity, forecast risk, and show the impact of priority or resourcing changes—so plans are realistic and defensible.

Does Jellyfish require time tracking?

No. Jellyfish infers work automatically from event-level signals across your existing tools without interrupting developer workflows.

How is Jellyfish different from dashboards or reports?

Jellyfish doesn’t just report metrics. It surfaces personalized signals, alerts, and insights so managers know where to act.
