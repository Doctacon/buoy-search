# Calculating the ROI of Engineering Teams

Measuring impact through ROI can give leadership context and visibility into the engineering teams’ direct impact to the business, but measuring this has traditionally been difficult. Why is measuring ROI of the engineering team so complex, and how can we simplify this process in order to rationalize strategic decisions?

Watch now to learn about the benefits that can be seen from measuring ROI across various areas, and how to use ROI to make better informed strategic decisions.

###### Other topics include:

- Determining how to use value proxies when measuring growth and impact
- How to measure investment costs
- Applying ROI to maximize key business decisions
