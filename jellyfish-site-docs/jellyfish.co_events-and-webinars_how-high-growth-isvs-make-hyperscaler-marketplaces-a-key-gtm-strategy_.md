# How High Growth ISVs Make Hyperscaler Marketplaces A Key GTM Strategy

Cloud marketplaces are booming, growing over 100% year over year, and representing a massive disruption to the way independent software vendors (ISVs) go to market. Almost [two-thirds of companies are already procuring through this rapidly growing channel](https://www.battery.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Battery-Ventures-State-of-Cloud-Software-Spending-Report-March-2023.pdf), and we’re only at the beginning. Cloud marketplace sales are predicted to hit $45 billion by 2025 as software buyers look to consolidate vendors, centralize billing, streamline procurement, and find alternative funding.

Hear from [Pranay Ahlawat](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ahlawatpranay/) (Partner and Associate Director at Boston Consulting Group), [Josh Greene](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joshgreene/) (Sr. Manager AWS Marketplace Development), and [Erik Peterson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/erikpeterson/) (Founder & CTO/CISO at CloudZero) as they discuss:

- Why cloud marketplaces are such a critical piece of software companies’ go-to-market (GTM) strategy
- How software buyers and sellers can capitalize on the Marketplace opportunity to accelerate time to value and innovate faster
- What best practices you can start replicating today to build a GTM motion with compounding effects
