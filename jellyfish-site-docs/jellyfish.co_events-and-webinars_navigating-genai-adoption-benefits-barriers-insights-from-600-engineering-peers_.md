AI has been top of mind in every organization in 2024, and engineering is no exception. More than six in 10 respondents (61%) to the 2024 State of Engineering Management Report say their team has embraced AI technologies to augment their engineering practices. However, there’s a disconnect between leadership and front-line workers: 76% of executives believe their team has embraced AI, while only 52% of engineers say the same.

On July 30, 2024, Chris Cox (CTO at KellerWilliams), Shane Sweeney (SVP, Technology at FanDuel), and Vidisha Suman (Partner at Kearney) reviewed invaluable insights from the 2024 State of Engineering Management Report and shared how executives and engineering teams can bridge the gap in measuring and managing AI adoption.
