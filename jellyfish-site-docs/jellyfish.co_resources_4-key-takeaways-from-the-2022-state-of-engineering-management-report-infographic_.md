# 4 Key Takeaways from the 2022 State of Engineering Management Report

The Jellyfish Research team analyzed the work of over 23,000 engineers, while surveying hundreds of engineering leaders to help us paint a picture of where engineering teams are investing their time and resources, how they are operating and performing, and the way in which leaders are managing today.

In this infographic, we highlight the data behind 4 key takeaways from the 2022 State of Engineering Management Report.
