## Turning AI Adoption into Actionable Insights

With Augment fully integrated into Jellyfish AI Impact, Augment usage and impact data is surfaced directly inside the Jellyfish platform, giving engineering leaders unprecedented visibility into how AI is being used and where it’s delivering results.

> Engineering leaders are investing heavily in AI, but most lack visibility into whether those investments are actually delivering ROI. By combining Augment’s industry-leading Context Engine with Jellyfish’s AI Impact telemetry, we’re giving engineering organizations the data they need to measure productivity gains, quantify business impact, and scale AI adoption with confidence. This moves AI from perceived productivity to trusted business impact.

_– Matt McClernan, CEO, Augment Code_

With Augment and Jellyfish, engineering leaders can:

- **Get instant, near real-time insights:** Powered by telemetry from the Augment Code API, Jellyfish surfaces up-to-date insights on AI adoption, usage, performance, and more, all within the Jellyfish platform.
- **Quantify AI’s impact on developer productivity:** AI Impact makes it possible to measure how Augment influences key engineering workflows – from building new features and reviewing code to reducing technical debt, improving code quality, and more.
- **Scale AI effectively across the organization:** AI Impact identifies teams with high AI adoption and strong outcomes, enabling leaders to replicate best practices and accelerate successful deployment of Augment across the entire engineering org.
