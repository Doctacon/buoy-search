Join us to discuss key findings from the 2025 State of Engineering Management Report and uncover how AI is transforming productivity and development. We surveyed over 600 R&D leaders who shared common trends, challenges, and tools shaping the future of engineering management.

We’ll hone in on **Human vs. AI Growth** and how the survey findings indicate that in most cases we’re not ready to radically transform our engineering organizations. Our discussion will focus on:

- How is AI shifting your hiring plans?
- How will that change over the next 1-2 years?
- What will the future partnership between human and AI work look like?

These insights will help you prepare for the hybrid AI-human future of engineering. Hope to see you there!
