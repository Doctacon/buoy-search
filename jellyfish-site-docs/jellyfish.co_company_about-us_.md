# Transforming developer productivity

Jellyfish combines the industry’s deepest engineering dataset with context-rich intelligence, so R&D teams can see what is driving impact and make smarter decisions across AI adoption, delivery, and engineering performance.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Group-4638-5.webp)

## Our history

2017

Founding Jellyfish

As R&D leaders, our founders, Andrew, David and Phil, experienced first-hand how difficult it was to communicate the value of and business impact delivered by engineering teams. With Jellyfish, they set out to change that.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JF-2020-1.webp)

2020

$12M Series A Raise

Jellyfish raises our first round of institutional funding to continue growing our team and executing on our product vision. In 2021 and 2022 we go on to our raise our B and C rounds, putting our total funding at $114.5M.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JF-2020-series-A.webp)

2023

Inaugural Company Kickoff

We bring the entire team together in person in our home city of Boston for the very first time.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JF-2023.webp)

2023

New Boston Office

Continued growth means a brand new office space in our hometown. It’s become a hub not just for local Jellies, but remote team members to gather, share and learn from one another.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JF-2023-new-office.webp)

2024

AI Impact Dashboard

Jellyfish becomes the first platform to launch a solution specifically built to measure the impact of AI coding tools.

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/JF-2024.webp)

2026+

Looking Ahead at Jellyfish

We’re building out an industry-leading platform and team focused on helping engineering leaders drive productivity and deliver business value. We can’t wait for what’s still to come!

![](https://jellyfish.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/jf-careers-carousel-25-2.webp)
