# Slack Integration

Send proactive alerts and share insights in channels to unblock delivery.

#### Overview

Slack is where many engineering teams communicate day to day. Connecting Slack to Jellyfish makes it easy to reach engineers with timely prompts and keep DevEx programs moving without extra manual follow-up.

#### What this integration powers:

- Higher survey response rates: Automated survey DMs (initial + reminders) reduce chasing and help you gather representative feedback.
- Better coverage with less effort: Target the right engineers and nudge only non-responders, so you get signal without spamming teams.
- Cleaner program operations: Closure alerts help survey owners monitor participation and take quick actions (extend, add recipients, resend reminders).
- Faster DevEx insights: Collect qualitative feedback at scale, then use it to identify friction points and prioritize improvements.
