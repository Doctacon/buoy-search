Today I am excited to share that Jellyfish [AI Impact](https://jellyfish.co/platform/jellyfish-ai-impact/) now supports [Windsurf](https://windsurf.com/), a leader in AI-powered coding solutions, offering tools like the Windsurf Editor that blend collaborative, continuous AI assistance with developers’ workflows. Together, Windsurf and Jellyfish are changing how AI and humans work together.

![Jellyfish Windsurf Integration](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/image1-1024x640.png)

Developed by a team of researchers and engineers, Windsurf builds on a combination of recent advances in generative models along with world-class optimized deep learning to provide organizations around the world with top quality AI-based products at the lowest possible costs.

Windsurf supports over 70 languages and integrates with more than 40 Integrated Developer Environments (IDEs), enabling developers to build and deploy applications more efficiently no matter where they work.

Through our robust integration with Windsurf, Jellyfish AI Impact allows joint customers to:

- **Measure Windsurf’s impact on overall developer productivity:** Analyze how Windsurf solutions like Windsurf Editor impact different engineering workflows and use cases – whether it’s building new features, reducing technical debt, or reviewing for code quality and security improvements.
- **Scale Windsurf adoption across the organization:** Identify teams with high engagement to further accelerate development, automate testing, and streamline deployment, then replicate these best practices and implementation strategies company-wide.

**Kevin Lu, CTO of Windsurf**, shares, “We’re excited to share that Windsurf Analytics is now integrated into Jellyfish. Windsurf has always helped teams write software faster, but the question we hear most from engineering leaders is how they can quantify the impact of our AI tooling. While we’ve shared metrics like ‘Percentage of Code Written,’ getting the full picture goes beyond in-app data.”

Lu continues, “With this integration, Jellyfish’s software engineering intelligence platform adds a critical connection to Windsurf Analytics, giving customers a full timeline of our tool usage to then correlate with other key software development metrics and measure value.”

## Building AI-Integrated Engineering Teams, Together

At Jellyfish, we know that engineering teams and business leaders alike want more than faster development, they want to understand how AI use is transforming workflows and ultimately, delivering business value. Windsurf provides the tools to get it done and Jellyfish provides the telemetry, observability, and strategic context to bridge the gap between experimentation and sustainable business outcomes.

AI integration marks a new chapter in software development, redefining how software is built, measured, and managed. Together, Jellyfish and Windsurf are delivering on the promise of the AIDLC so engineering organizations and businesses as a whole can move forward with the data, insights and clarity they need to succeed.
