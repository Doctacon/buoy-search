# 5 Jira Best Practices for Improving Your Engineering Operations

#### Optimize your process and reporting in Jira with these 5 best practices for engineering operations and software development leaders

Engineering leaders are constantly looking to improve on the operations of their team, improve efficiencies, and set their teams up for success. For teams that use Jira to track workflows and progress, it can be a powerful tool for finding insights, but it can also be complex and confusing for managing and improving engineering operations. It’s tough to know where to start in organizing Jira to work well for your organization.

In this e-book you’ll learn the best practices that teams use to organize their work and optimize their workflows in Jira. Guide your team to adopt these best practices and you’ll be able to significantly improve visibility and reporting, and take steps to optimize your organization’s engineering operations.
