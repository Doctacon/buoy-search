For the sixth consecutive quarter, the Jellyfish Engineering Management Platform has been recognized as a Leader within [the Software Development Analytics Tools Grid](https://www.g2.com/categories/software-development-analytics-tools#grid) by [G2](https://www.g2.com/), the world’s largest and most trusted software marketplace.

We earned the recognition by receiving positive reviews from verified users compared to similar products in the same category. For inclusion in the report a product must have received 10 or more reviews. Holding a Leader position for six reports in a row is quite the accomplishment, as you must be receiving new positive reviews on a regular basis to stay ahead in the grid. [Download your own copy of the Winter 2024 G2 Grid Report.](https://www.jellyfish.co/resources/g2-grid-report-winter-2024/)

We are also proud to announce that we have been named a leader within the Americas, Europe, and EMEA Regional Grid Reports. These reports are curated by only including location-relevant data to aggregate standard scores. In the instance of these three reports, the region is determined by where the reviewer was at the time of publishing.

Jellyfish has also maintained a leader role within the Mid-Market Relationship Index for Software Development Analytics Tools. Jellyfish was the only company to receive a score greater than 9 (9.55/10). The relationship index is calculated using four factors:

- Customers’ reported ease of doing business with the seller based on reviews by G2 users
- Customers’ satisfaction with the product’s quality of support based on reviews by G2 users
- Customers’ likelihood to recommend each product based on reviews by G2 users
- The number of reviews received on G2; buyers trust a product with more reviews, and a greater number of reviews indicates a more representative and accurate reflection of the customer experience.
