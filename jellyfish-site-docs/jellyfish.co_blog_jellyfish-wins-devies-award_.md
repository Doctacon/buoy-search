Jellyfish

February 15, 2023

We’re excited to announce that our Engineering Management Platform has won a [2023 DEVIES Award](https://www.developerweek.com/awards/) in the Enterprise Development & Integration Solutions category.

The awards recognize outstanding design, engineering, and innovation in developer technology. We’d like to thank the DevNetwork team and all of our supporters who helped make this possible. We would not be receiving this award without you.

As Jonathan Pasky, executive producer of DevNetwork, producer of DeveloperWeek and the 2023 DEVIES Awards, said, “Developer tools and technology product solutions are leading the way for software developers and engineers to build upon the foundation of the ever-expanding technology sector. Jellyfish’s win is evidence of their leading role in the growth and innovation in the software industry.”

The DEVIES Award winners, selected from a record-high 310 nominations, were chosen for attracting significant attention in the software industry, gaining a following among the developer community, and being a technical leader in their sector for innovation.

Jellyfish will receive its award during [DeveloperWeek 2023](https://www.developerweek.com/), the world’s largest developer conference with more than 8,000 participants from over 150 countries across the globe.

This recognition is a testament to our team’s hard work helping customers achieve their engineering goals and align their work to business objectives. We recently added [Life Cycle Explorer](https://www.jellyfish.co/blog/jellyfish-life-cycle-explorer-announcement/), which lets teams identify and resolve the root cause of bottlenecks in the software development process, to our platform — and there’s much more to come in 2023.

Thanks for joining us on this ride.
