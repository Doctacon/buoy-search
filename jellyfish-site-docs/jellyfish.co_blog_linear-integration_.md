Today we’re excited to share that Jellyfish now integrates with [Linear](https://linear.app/), the modern product development platform for high-velocity software teams, giving Linear customers the ability to see their work alongside key engineering productivity metrics directly in the Jellyfish platform.

![Jellyfish_Linear_Integration](https://test-jellyfish-co.pantheonsite.io/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Jellyfish_Linear_Integration.png)

This new partnership gives R&D leaders and their teams a better view of how software development work is allocated and whether teams are focused on priority projects, as well as the ability to more easily identify bottlenecks, eliminate friction and ship products faster.

“Our partnership with Jellyfish brings Linear customers the instrumentation required to take their development teams to the next level,” said Cristina Cordova, COO of Linear. “We’re excited to partner with Jellyfish to give leaders greater visibility into how work gets done and empower them to build more efficiently.”

Linear’s focus on modernizing development practices matches Jellyfish’s mission of providing better visibility to R&D teams, especially as AI tooling has become a critical part of the software development lifecycle. The joint solution gives developers and engineering leaders the tools they need to better plan and allocate work while ensuring teams are aligned to key business priorities and efficiently delivering products to market.

Key integration capabilities include:

- Detailed dashboards to surface trends and insights, giving team leads the ability to take action and report performance to executive stakeholders
- Visibility into work allocation across teams and individuals to spot bottlenecks and areas for improvement
- Ability to visualize investment levels by project, enabling leaders to understand whether teams are aligned to business priorities
- Industry-standard engineering productivity metrics like DORA, as well as delivery forecasts to pinpoint bottlenecks and course-correct as needed
- AI-driven work categorization, providing even companies with “messy data” access to automated accurate data analysis and insights
- Access to Jellyfish [AI Impact](https://jellyfish.co/platform/jellyfish-ai-impact/), giving engineering leaders the data and guidance to measure AI progress, adapt quickly, and deliver lasting business impact

Together, Jellyfish and Linear are modernizing software development practices by providing one source of truth for workflows and metrics.
