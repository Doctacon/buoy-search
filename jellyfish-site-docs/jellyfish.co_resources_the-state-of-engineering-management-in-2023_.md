# The State of Engineering Management in 2023

With the unsteady economic climate, 2022 saw a shift from growth at all costs to efficient growth heading into 2023. How are engineering leaders interpreting direction from the board and their executives, and how are they taking action to prepare their teams for success in light of an uncertain time?

For the fourth consecutive year, Jellyfish surveyed thousands of engineering teams to better understand trends across three key areas. Read the State of Engineering Management in 2023 to unpack:

- Where engineering teams are investing their time
- What challenges teams are facing on an operational level
- Notable differences between underperforming and overachieving teams
- Download the report to learn more about the state of engineering management in 2023.
