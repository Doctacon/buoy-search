In this article

The world of AI-driven software development is evolving at a breakneck pace – tools, models, frameworks, and best practices are changing by the week, sometimes by the day. At Jellyfish, we’re not just watching this transformation – we’re diving in headfirst.

This blog is your curated guide through the whirlwind. Each issue will bring you a handpicked collection of the most important news, insightful articles, practical tools, and helpful resources in the world of AI-assisted coding and development. You’ll also get commentary from the team here at Jellyfish – what we’re exploring, what we’re building, and how we’re thinking about the intersection of AI and engineering workflows.

Whether you’re experimenting with AI pair programming, thinking about how LLMs will reshape your team’s velocity, or just trying to keep up – we’ve got you covered. So be sure to follow along!
