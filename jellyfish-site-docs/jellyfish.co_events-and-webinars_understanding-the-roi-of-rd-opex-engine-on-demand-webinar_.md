# Understanding the ROI of R&D

In this webinar Andrew Lau, Co-Founder and CEO of Jellyfish, and Lauren Kelley, CEO of OPEXEngine discuss how engineering executives can use benchmarks to effectively and efficiently communicate their business impact. They cover the SaaS R&D expense maturity curve and discuss how Engineering Management Platforms could be the new CRM.

###### In this webinar, you’ll learn:

- How engineering’s full team efforts impact bottom-line growth
- How to align engineering KPIs with business KPIs
- How to use data to guide product strategy and long-term business plans
