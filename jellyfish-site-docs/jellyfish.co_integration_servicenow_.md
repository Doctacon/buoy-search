# ServiceNow Integration

Sync ServiceNow service requests, change requests, and problem records to measure operational workload and performance metrics.

#### Overview

ServiceNow is an IT service management platform used to track incidents, service delivery work, and operational processes across support teams.

#### What this integration powers:

- Data Hub visibility into ITSM trends like incident volume, resolution speeds, and change success rates
- Operational workload reporting by tracking ticket aging, state transitions, and volume distribution across assignment groups to identify bottlenecks
- Reliability context for engineering reporting by bringing incident and change signals into Jellyfish alongside development delivery
