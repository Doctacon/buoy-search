# How Acoustic Uses Jellyfish Benchmarks for Contractor Savings

Benchmarking features have become instrumental within engineering management, allowing for more informed and accurate decision-making using relevant metrics and high-performance data. With comprehensive and comparative indicators, engineering leaders are not only able to understand their teams’ performance, but also gain visibility into how it compares to industry standards.

In this live webinar, John Riewerts, Senior Vice President of Engineering, Acoustic, and Milan Thakker, Product Manager, Jellyfish, discuss the importance of benchmarking within your engineering team and how Jellyfish provides benchmarks for informed decision making.
