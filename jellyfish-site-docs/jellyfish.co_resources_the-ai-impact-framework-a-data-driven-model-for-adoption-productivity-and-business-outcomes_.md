# The AI Impact Framework

### A Data-Driven Model for Adoption, Productivity & Business Outcomes

Engineering leaders are facing a new challenge: proving AI is actually delivering impact. Krishna Kannan, Head of Product at Jellyfish, covers the Jellyfish AI Impact Framework – built in collaboration with 300+ engineering leaders and grounded in data from over 20,000 developers.

Learn how to assess adoption, productivity, and business outcomes with a structure tailored to today’s hybrid AI-human development teams.
