A More Actionable Dashboard for Engineering Managers

## A More Actionable Dashboard for Engineering Managers

Team Pulse is an intuitive dashboard designed to help engineering managers quickly assess team health, resolve blockers, and manage delivery risk. Instead of navigating multiple views or cobbling together disparate data, Team Pulse offers a unified lens into three core areas:

1. **Priorities:** Monitors top epics, team allocation, and progress in real time. Dive deeper into individual epics to understand blockers or scope changes.
2. **Unblock Work:** Automatically flags stuck work – like Pull Requests open for more than two days or issues idle for more than three – allowing engineering managers to take timely action.
3. **Focus:** Gives a readout of “High Risk” vs. “Low Risk” for key metrics across _Epics per_ _Engineer_ (Is your team spread too thin?) and _Priority Focus_ (Are your resources going to your top priorities?)

Team Pulse puts actionable intelligence in the hands of the people who need it most – engineering managers responsible for unblocking developers and guiding execution.

With Team Pulse, engineering managers can:

- Predict delays before they escalate
- Quickly identify and address blockers
- Maintain focus on high-priority initiatives
- Keep their teams aligned, motivated, and productive

## Lead Your Team Effectively, One Pulse Check at a Time

Whether you’re prepping for a standup, sprint planning, or just checking in with your teams, Team Pulse delivers fast insights without requiring deep configuration.

Team Pulse is ideal when:

- You need a simplified version of traditional Jellyfish metrics
- Your team is struggling with visibility into current work
- You’re juggling multiple priorities and looking to reduce operational risk
