## Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jellyfish Operational Effectiveness?

Jellyfish Operational Effectiveness is a platform that helps engineering managers and platform teams understand where developers lose time, identify bottlenecks, and improve team performance using workflow data, people insights, and personalized recommendations.

How does Jellyfish help engineering managers improve team performance?

Jellyfish reveals patterns in team activity, highlights cross-team bottlenecks, and provides coaching insights so EMs can run better 1:1s, unblock developers earlier, and improve predictability across sprints.

How does Jellyfish identify bottlenecks in developer workflows?

Jellyfish analyzes signals across tools, pipelines, code activity, and developer feedback to surface emerging friction—whether in reviews, builds, planning, or handoffs—so managers and platform teams can intervene before issues spread.

What metrics does Jellyfish track for operational effectiveness?

Jellyfish tracks throughput, cycle time, review speed, flow metrics, delivery signals, team health indicators, sentiment data, and benchmarks—providing a holistic view of performance across individuals, teams, and orgs.

How is Jellyfish different from engineering analytics or DevEx survey tools?

Unlike tools that look only at metrics or only at surveys, Jellyfish unifies workflow data, people insights, platform signals, and context in one place. This gives EMs and platform teams clear, actionable guidance, not just dashboards.
