What the Spring 2025 Report Shows

## What the Spring 2025 Report Shows

In G2’s Spring 2025 Grid Report, we scored a 95 out of 100 in user satisfaction and a 9.41 out of 10 in the Mid-Market Relationship Index. These scores are more than just numbers – they tell us we’re on the right track when it comes to building a product that’s _actually_ useful.

The Mid-Market Relationship Index highlights where we’re making a real impact:

- **Ease of Doing Business:** Working with Jellyfish is simple and collaborative
- **Quality of Support:** Satisfaction with the quality of our customer support
- **Likelihood to Recommend:** 90% of users say they’d recommend us
- **Number of Reviews:** More reviews = more trust, showcasing a more accurate reflection of the Jellyfish customer experience
